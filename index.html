About Me

I'm a passionate UX/UI design student at Sushant University, Gurugram (2022-2026), with a love for graphic design, photo editing, and video editing. My creative journey began in 2016 with YouTube channels, exploring gaming content and honing editing skills. I've traveled to 23+ countries, drawing inspiration from diverse cultures and experiences. With strong interests in design and content creation, I strive to bring fresh, engaging experiences to everything I do.

Skills

I have hands-on experience with a range of creative tools to bring ideas to life. I use Figma for UX/UI design and prototyping, creating user-friendly and visually appealing digital experiences. For graphic design and photo editing, I work with Adobe Photoshop. In video editing, I use Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects to craft high-quality, dynamic video content. Additionally, I am skilled in mobile editing apps like CapCut, InShot, and Alight Motion, allowing me to create engaging short-form content and motion graphics efficiently.

Design Visit

Seoul, South Korea

Dongdaemun Design Plaza (DDP) in Seoul, South Korea, is a world-renowned hub for design, creativity, and innovation. Designed by Zaha Hadid, DDP is celebrated for its futuristic architecture, fluid forms, and immersive use of space and lighting. The plaza hosts a variety of exhibitions, design markets, fashion shows, and cultural events, making it a dynamic center for creative industries. Visiting DDP offers a unique experience where design, technology, and architecture blend seamlessly to inspire new ideas and forward-thinking concepts.

Tokyo, Japan

TeamLab Planets Tokyo is an immersive art museum where the boundaries between art, technology, and the human body dissolve. Created by the interdisciplinary art collective teamLab, the museum invites visitors to walk through water, move freely among monumental digital installations, and become part of the artwork itself. Through interactive light, sound, and space, teamLab Planets creates a deeply sensory and emotional experience that redefines how people engage with art. It stands as a global symbol of innovation, blending creativity and technology in a truly unforgettable way.
